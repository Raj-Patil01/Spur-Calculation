Project Group 35

Development of web based expert system for Designing of Spur, Helical, Bevel and Worm gear

About Project

If you're performing some engineering calculations often. That can be developed as an application (software) or webpage this engineering calculations can be solved faster than other processes. It eliminates the repeated work of design calculations and performs calculations precisely. The main aim is to minimize time required for designing the Gears to increase the productivity and accuracy in design. The webpage we developed, is quire the inputs for which the gear is to be design. It performs the design calculations and shows result on webpage. The expert base webpage calculates the design parameters of gears (module, addendum, dedendum, clearance, tooth thickness). The web page takes input from user and performs all design calculations. By using this expert-based system an unskilled person who has only basic knowledge of computer can perform the design calculations in minimum time. Interface of our web page is very user friendly and easy to understand any school or college level student can excess this web page easily its just like filling a form.

It is made of formulas of gear design theories (Beam strength equation, wear strength of gear). This system increases manufacturing rate of gear as no physical calculations are needed and decreases labor cost as it needs only one person to handle it which not necessarily requires skill. The web page automates the mechanical design process. The web page is application of programming knowledge in mechanical engineering.

Gears

SPUR GEAR

Spur gears are a cylindrical shaped toothed component used in industrial equipment to transfer mechanical motion as well as control speed, power, and torque. These simple gears are cost-effective, durable, reliable and provide a positive, constant speed drive to facilitate daily industrial operations.

Learn more

HELICAL GEAR

helical gears are one type of cylindrical gears with slanted tooth trace. Compared to spur gears, they have the larger contact ratio and excel in quietness and less vibration and able to transmit large force. A pair of helical gears has the same helix angle but the helix hand is opposite.

Learn more

BEVEL GEAR

Bevel gears are gears where the axes of the two shafts intersect and the tooth-bearing faces of the gears themselves are conically shaped. Bevel gears are most often mounted on shafts that are 90 degrees apart, but can be designed to work at other angles as well.

Learn more

WORM GEAR

A worm drive is a gear arrangement in which a worm (which is a gear in the form of a screw) meshes with a worm wheel (which is similar in appearance to a spur gear). The two elements are also called the worm screw and worm gear.
